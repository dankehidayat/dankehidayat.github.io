Hyprland Automated Bootstrap Installer

The Hyprland Automated Bootstrap Installer streamlines the setup process for your Hyprland environment. This script not only installs essential packages but also automatically copies your personalized dotfiles, ensuring your system is configured exactly to your preferences. Additionally, it offers an optional feature to apply a custom GRUB theme, giving your bootloader a polished, unique appearance. With a focus on efficiency and automation, this installer minimizes setup time and maximizes convenience for setting up a fully functional, customized Hyprland desktop environment.
