# Solving Differential Equations

## Simple Harmonic Motion

$$
\frac{d^2x}{dt^2} + \omega^2 x = 0
$$

Solution:

$$
x(t) = A\cos(\omega t) + B\sin(\omega t)
$$

## Heat Equation

$$
\frac{\partial u}{\partial t} = \alpha \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2}
$$
