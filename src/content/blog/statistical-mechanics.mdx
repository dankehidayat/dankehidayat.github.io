# Statistical Mechanics and Probability

## Boltzmann Distribution

$$
P_i = \frac{e^{-E_i/kT}}{Z}
$$

Where partition function:

$$
Z = \sum_i e^{-E_i/kT}
$$

## Entropy

$$
S = -k \sum_i P_i \ln P_i
$$

## Ideal Gas Law

From microscopic perspective:

$$
PV = NkT
$$
