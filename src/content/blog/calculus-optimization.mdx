# Optimization with Calculus

## Gradient Descent

The update rule:

$$
\theta_{t+1} = \theta_t - \eta \nabla J(\theta_t)
$$

Where $\eta$ is the learning rate.

## Partial Derivatives

For a function $f(x,y)$:

$$
\frac{\partial f}{\partial x} = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h,y) - f(x,y)}{h}
$$
