# Number Theory in Cryptography

## RSA Encryption

Public key: $(n, e)$ where $n = pq$

Private key: $d$ where $ed \equiv 1 \pmod{\phi(n)}$

Encryption: $c = m^e \mod n$

Decryption: $m = c^d \mod n$

## Euler's Theorem

If $\gcd(a,n) = 1$, then:

$$
a^{\phi(n)} \equiv 1 \pmod{n}
$$
